NCT 127 is NCT's Seoul team and consists of 10 members, Taeyong, Taeil, Utah, Jaehyun, Winwin, Mark, Hachan, Doyoung, Johnny, and Jungwoo, who are from various nationalities including Korea, China, Japan, and Canada. The number 127 in the team's name refers to the longitude of Seoul, signifying that the team will be based in Seoul, the home of K-POP, and will perform on stages around the world.
